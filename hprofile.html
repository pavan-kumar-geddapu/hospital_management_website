Our Solution Highlights.

Patient Management

Previous patient records
  • transform operations through single screen flows

  • Make automated appointment reminder calls/texts/emails
  • Patient portal for self-service

Clinical Management

  • Coherent longitudinal summary views
  • Computerized Physician Order Entry (CPOE)
  • Comprehensive screens and graphical representation of vitals
  • User definable clinical documentation template
  • Clinical decision support system with coded diagnosis and procedures
  • Record, update and archive electronic medical records

  • Ability to dynamically create, update and assign packages

  • Comprehensive, hands-off credit and collections monitoring
  • Finance Business Intelligence Tools for transforming the planning process
