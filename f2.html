Ambulance


GMR Varalakshmi Care Hospital offers 24x7 Ambulance Facility comprising of two ambulances. One is state-of-the-art advanced care ambulance and other is the basic ambulance. Advanced care ambulance, meant for transportation of unstable patients, is equipped with defibrillator, multipara monitor, ventilator, oxygen, suction, scoop stretcher, spine board, head immobilizer, spencer trolley stretcher, emergency drugs etc. This ambulance is manned by ACLS / BLS trained doctors, nurses along with trained drivers and attendants. Basic ambulance, equipped with oxygen, is meant for transportation of stable patients. It is manned by skilled attendants and drivers.
