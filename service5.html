Dialysis


Dialysis unit was inaugurated on 12-Jan-2015. We have installed 03 dialysis equipment to cater the needs of heamo dialysis patients in and around Rajam. It is run with a full time nephrologist, dialysis technicians and supported by nursing team to run the facility for 12 hours daily. The facility is helping six patients daily on an average.
