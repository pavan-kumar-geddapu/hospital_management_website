Canteen


The hospital Canteen is located in the hospital to serve the patients, attendants and visitors. The hygienic food served in the canteen at affordable rates is found very useful for all those receiving Healthcare services from the Hospital. The canteen is open for services between 5.30 AM and 9.30 PM. GMR Varalakshmi CARE Hospital has a well equipped canteen which is designed to serve wholesome and hygienic meals to our patients and attendants. Our canteen uses the best equipment and minimizes human contact with food items for the highest levels of hygiene.
